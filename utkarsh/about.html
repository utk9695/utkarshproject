ABOUT GRAND MARIAN
 
For your stay in Ludhiana, premier city of Punjab where you may visit on business, social or family visit. Hotel GRAND MARIAN offers you an homely atmosphere and comfortable accommodation. Air conditioned, Air cooled Rooms with modern amenities are available at a reasonable tariff. It also boast to be Ludhiana's first hotel to provide Internet access to it's customers. Banquet and Conference Hall, a recent addition to our hotel is popular for Marriage Ceremonies, Parties,Function's, Exhibitions, Sale Fairs and Business Conferences. So is our Indian and Chinese catering service. Hotel GRAND MARIAN is well located, near main Shopping and Commercial Centers. Easy to reach from Railway Station and Bus Stations. Reservation/Enquiries on room availability can be requested by post, fax, phone, email or through the Online Booking Form.

Hotel GRAND MARIAN Ludhiana is one of the best hotels in ludhiana. Service with a Smile, Affection and Care is the main Motto of Hotel GRAND MARIAN, Ludhiana.Hotel GRAND MARIAN offers all travel services - tours booking, hotels booking, car rentals etc.

The Accommodation at Hotel GRAND MARIAN includes Well Appointed, Elegantly Decorated Rooms with a Relaxing Ambience.

All the Rooms are with Attached Bath, LCD Television and a Direct Dialing Telephone.The Relaxing Decor is Further Complemented with the 24 Hour Room Service to add to your Comfort and Convenience.

Our Rooms and Suites are Spacious and Provide the Comforts of Home to Travelers.
